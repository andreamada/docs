## **What is WaaS and how does it work?**

The WaaS (Website-as-a-Service) allows you to build and Sell pre-Made WordPress Sites, create site templates and offer them as turnkey solutions, Automate Sales and Deployment.\
It's not a multi-site solution, each installation is independent and it's powered by DirectAdmin control panel behind.
