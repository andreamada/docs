## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrade / Downgrade

1. Click **Change Plan** on the Billing page
2. Browse available plans and their features
3. Select a new plan
4. Confirm the change

**Upgrades** take effect immediately. The difference in price is prorated for the remainder of the current period.

**Downgrades** take effect at the start of the next billing period. You keep full access to the current plan until then.

### Cancel Subscription

1. Click **Cancel Subscription**
2. Review what happens after cancellation (access until period end)
3. Optionally provide a cancellation reason
4. Confirm

Your subscription remains **Active** until the end of the current period. After that, access is downgraded or removed based on platform settings.

<Info>
Canceling a subscription does not delete your sites or data immediately. Contact support to recover data after cancellation if needed.
</Info>

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Card (Stripe)

1. Click **Add Payment Method**
2. Enter your card details in the Stripe-hosted form
3. Card is saved securely by Stripe (AreaWP never stores raw card numbers)
4. New card becomes the default for future renewals

### Updating a Card

Replace an expired or invalid card:

1. Click **Update Payment Method**
2. Enter new card details
3. Save — future renewals use the new card

### Bank Transfer

If your platform uses Bank Transfer as a payment method, you'll see your invoices with payment instructions. After sending the transfer, contact support to have your payment confirmed.

## Billing Help

Having billing issues?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment failed">
    If your payment failed, check that your card is valid and has sufficient funds. Update your payment method and click **Retry Payment** to attempt the charge again immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Subscription suspended">
    If your subscription is suspended, add a valid payment method and click **Reactivate Subscription**. Your sites and data are preserved during suspension.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Request a refund">
    Contact support with your invoice number to request a refund. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days for card payments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Need a VAT invoice">
    Ensure your billing details include your company name and VAT number under **Account → Profile**. Future invoices will include this information automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
